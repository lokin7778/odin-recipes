FRIED RICE RECIPE


DESCRIPTION OF THE RECIPE

This recipe focuses on making vegetable fried rice which is high in fiber and healthy, all in under 30 mins which can be a great option for any meal time.

The image above is a representation of what the final product might look like. This recipe will guide you to make the dish more healthy, while maintaining a good taste.

INGREDIENTS

  • Oil (2tsp)
  • Cooked Rice (350gms)
  • Green beans (50gms)
  • Carrot (50gms)
  • Bell Pepper (50gms)
  • Soy Sauce (1-1.5 tsp)
  • Salt
  • Chilli Vinegar (1.5-2 tsp)
  • Green Onions (10gms)
  • Sweet Corn (80gms)
  • If you want it spicy, Schezwan Sauce (2 tsp)

STEPS

  1. Heat the oil in a pan for about 30-40 seconds and add minced garlic to it. Saute till the color changes.
  2. After this, add all of your chopped vegetables to the pan and saute for about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Now add half of the given content of soy sauce and half of the given content of the chilli vinegar and saute for a minute more.
  4. Next, add the rice to the contents in the pan and add the rest of the soy sacue, chilli vinegar and salt too and saute for 2-3 minutes.
  5. If you choose to add the Schezwan Sauce, now's a good time. Add the Schezwan sauce to your pan and saute for a minute more.
  6. Finally, top off your fried rice with some chopped green onions and voila! Your healthy and tasty fried rice is ready!
