OATS SHAKE RECIPE

DESCRIPTION OF THE RECIPE

This is by far one of the best recipes that I have created on my own which is serving me as my breakfast for the past 2 years. It is one of the most healthiest and protein-filled meals.

This can be prepared in under 2 minutes, so a really good breakfast option when it comes to chaotic mornings. It can keep you full for about 5 hours as it is filled with fibre in the form of Oats!

INGREDIENTS

  • ROLLED OATS (100 gms)
  • 2% REDUCED FAT MILK (500 mL)
  • Honey (1 tbsp)

STEPS TO PREPARE

  1. Take 100 gms of rolled oats and put in a blender and blend it for about 30 seconds, which turns the oats into a fine powder.
  2. Then measure 500 mL of the reduced fat milk and pour it into the blender in which you have the powdered oats already blended.
  3. Blend the mixture of powdered oats and the milk for 30 seconds as to make the shake more liquid in consistency and not really lumpy.
  4. Voila! Your high protein high fibre oats shake is ready to be served!
